##  Purchasing & Access Issues

### Can't access purchased files / no orders showing

**Problem:** Purchased files but the download area shows no orders.

**Fix:** Download links are sent to the email used at checkout. Check spam/junk folder. If ordered as a guest without an account, check email. Contact Flightory at contact@flightory.com — they respond gradually due to high email volume.

---

### Painful slow downloads that keep failing
**Fix (from Flightory):** Check if your browser is blocking pop-ups, which may prevent the download. Try a different browser, device, or network (e.g. mobile data). Email Flightory if nothing helps.

---

### T-Motor website — how to pay

**Problem:** T-Motor allows ordering without payment and gives no clear payment instructions.

**Community answer:** T-Motor also has an **AliExpress store** which is more reliable and straightforward to purchase from. Amazon sometimes carries T-Motor products from third-party sellers. Email T-Motor directly to ask about PayPal.

---

### Can't place order on Flightory website

**Fix:** Contact Flightory directly: https://flightory.com/contact
