# 🌍 04 – Community Projects & Knowledge

This folder is for **you** — the community. Share your own Flightory builds, findings, guides, and questions here.

Whether you're a student, hobbyist, or experienced builder, your experience is valuable to others.

---

## 📂 What You'll Find Here

- Personal build reports and project showcases
- Community-sourced guides and how-tos
- Open questions and answers
- Findings from Facebook, Discord, Reddit, and beyond

---

## 🤝 Contribute

This folder lives and grows through community contributions. Feel free to:

- Open an **Issue** to ask a question or suggest a topic
- Submit a **Pull Request** with your own report or guide
- Share findings from the Flightory Facebook group, Discord, or Reddit

Please include a source reference with each entry:

```
Source: Personal experience, tested on YYYY-MM-DD
Source: Discord, User XY, post from YYYY-MM-DD
Source: Facebook Group "Flightory Community", link: …
```
